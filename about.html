Victor and Yaneth

Victor and Yaneth had been living in Greer for several years and saw the need for the Hispanic community to have a place where the same language was spoken to them and where their needs were fully met. Motivated by this great need, on November 27, Mundo Latino Multiservicios opened its doors to that beautiful and brave Hispanic community and to anyone who needed our services.
